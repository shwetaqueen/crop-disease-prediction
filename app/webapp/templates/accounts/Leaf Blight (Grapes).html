Crop Disease Organic Solutions

Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
Leaf Blight (Grapes) Irregular brown lesions on leaves, leading to defoliation and reduced fruit yield.
  • Prune infected leaves to improve airflow and reduce fungal spread.
  • Apply copper-based fungicides as an organic treatment.
  • Use neem oil spray to prevent further infections.
  • Ensure proper drainage to avoid excess moisture buildup.
  • Introduce Trichoderma-based biofungicides to suppress fungal spores.
  • Spray compost tea to enhance plant resistance.
  • Use a mixture of baking soda and water as a natural fungicide.
  • Encourage beneficial insects that help reduce fungal spores.
  • Mulch around the base to prevent soil splash contamination.
  • Avoid overhead irrigation to keep leaves dry.
  • Rotate crops and avoid planting in previously infected areas.
  • Apply potassium bicarbonate spray to slow disease progression.
  • Use sulfur-based organic fungicides to manage blight naturally.
