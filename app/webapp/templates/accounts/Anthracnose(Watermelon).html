Crop Disease Organic Solutions

Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
Anthracnose (Watermelon) Sunken, dark lesions on fruits and leaves.
  • Use copper-based fungicides.
  • Apply neem oil spray.
  • Ensure good air circulation and avoid overhead watering.
  • Use organic sulfur spray.
  • Apply compost tea.
  • Use a mix of baking soda and water.
  • Spray a garlic extract solution.
  • Plant resistant watermelon varieties.
  • Use mulch around plants.
  • Apply a biofungicide containing Trichoderma.
  • Maintain crop rotation.
  • Introduce beneficial microbes.
  • Regularly remove infected leaves and fruits.
