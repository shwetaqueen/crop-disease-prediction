Crop Disease Organic Solutions

Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
Potato Late Blight Irregular dark brown or black lesions on leaves, white fungal growth on undersides, rapid plant wilting, and tuber rot.
  • Apply copper-based organic fungicides every 7–10 days.
  • Use a neem oil spray to suppress fungal spores.
  • Ensure proper spacing for good air circulation and reduced humidity.
  • Remove and destroy infected plant parts to prevent disease spread.
  • Use resistant potato varieties to reduce vulnerability.
  • Spray a mixture of baking soda and water as a preventive measure.
  • Avoid overhead watering; use drip irrigation instead.
  • Apply compost tea to enhance plant immunity.
  • Use a garlic extract solution to inhibit fungal growth.
  • Rotate crops with non-host plants like beans or cereals.
  • Introduce biofungicides containing Trichoderma.
  • Mulch with organic materials to prevent soil splash.
  • Monitor plants regularly, especially in humid conditions.
