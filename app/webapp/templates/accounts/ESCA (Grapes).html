Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
ESCA (Grapes) Interveinal chlorosis, tiger-striped leaves, wood decay, and sudden wilting.
  • Prune infected vines and burn affected plant material.
  • Apply beneficial fungi like Trichoderma to infected wood.
  • Use organic biofungicides to reduce fungal spread.
  • Ensure proper vineyard drainage to prevent root infections.
  • Spray neem oil to slow disease progression.
  • Improve soil health with compost and organic fertilizers.
  • Encourage mycorrhizal fungi to strengthen root systems.
  • Apply a garlic extract spray for antifungal properties.
  • Use copper-based fungicides as a preventive measure.
  • Practice clean pruning techniques and sterilize tools.
  • Increase vineyard airflow by maintaining proper vine spacing.
  • Introduce natural biocontrol agents like Bacillus subtilis.
  • Mulch around vines to retain soil moisture and prevent stress.
