Crop Disease Organic Solutions

Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
Tomato Leaf Mold Yellow spots on upper leaf surfaces, velvety olive-green mold on undersides, and leaf curling.
  • Ensure proper ventilation and avoid overcrowding plants.
  • Water at the base to prevent excess moisture on leaves.
  • Use a baking soda spray (1 tsp baking soda + 1 liter of water).
  • Apply neem oil spray to inhibit fungal growth.
  • Use a copper-based fungicide as a preventive measure.
  • Remove infected leaves immediately to stop the spread.
  • Apply a compost tea spray to strengthen plant immunity.
  • Use sulfur-based organic fungicides.
  • Encourage beneficial microbes in the soil.
  • Rotate crops yearly to prevent pathogen buildup.
  • Introduce biofungicides containing Trichoderma.
  • Mulch around the base to maintain proper soil moisture.
