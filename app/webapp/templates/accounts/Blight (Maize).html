Crop Disease Organic Solutions

Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
Blight (Maize) Yellow-brown streaks on leaves, premature leaf drying, reduced grain yield.
  • Apply a compost tea spray.
  • Use a mixture of garlic extract and water.
  • Rotate crops regularly.
  • Spray neem oil weekly.
  • Introduce Trichoderma-based biofungicides.
  • Use copper-based organic fungicides.
  • Ensure proper field drainage to prevent moisture buildup.
  • Remove and burn infected plant debris.
  • Plant disease-resistant maize varieties.
  • Encourage natural predators like ladybugs to reduce fungal spread.
  • Avoid excessive nitrogen fertilization.
  • Mulch around plants to suppress fungal spores.
  • Spray a mixture of baking soda and vegetable oil.
