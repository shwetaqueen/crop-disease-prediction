Tomato Late Blight Dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves and stems, white fungal growth on undersides of leaves, and rapid fruit rot.
  • Apply copper-based fungicides to control fungal spread.
  • Use neem oil spray to reduce fungal spores.
  • Remove and destroy infected leaves and fruits to prevent disease spread.
  • Improve airflow by ensuring proper plant spacing.
  • Use a mixture of baking soda and water as a preventive spray.
  • Apply a compost tea spray to enhance plant immunity.
  • Mulch around plants to reduce soil splash contamination.
  • Use potassium bicarbonate spray to suppress fungal growth.
  • Water plants at the base to avoid moisture buildup on leaves.
  • Rotate crops annually to prevent pathogen buildup in the soil.
  • Introduce biofungicides containing Trichoderma for natural protection.
  • Encourage beneficial soil microbes to strengthen plant defenses.
