Potato Early Blight Dark brown spots with concentric rings on leaves, yellowing of lower leaves, and reduced tuber yield.
  • Apply copper-based organic fungicides every 7–10 days.
  • Use neem oil spray to suppress fungal growth.
  • Rotate crops with non-host plants like legumes or grains.
  • Ensure proper plant spacing for good airflow and reduce humidity.
  • Remove and destroy infected leaves to prevent disease spread.
  • Use compost tea as a foliar spray to enhance plant immunity.
  • Apply a baking soda and water mixture to slow fungal development.
  • Encourage beneficial microbes by adding organic mulch.
  • Use resistant potato varieties to minimize infection risk.
  • Avoid overhead watering; use drip irrigation instead.
  • Spray sulfur-based organic fungicides as a preventive measure.
  • Maintain balanced soil nutrients with organic fertilizers.
  • Apply a garlic extract solution to protect against fungal spores.
