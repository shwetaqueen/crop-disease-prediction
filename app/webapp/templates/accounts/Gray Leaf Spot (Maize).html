Gray Leaf Spot (Maize) Grayish, rectangular lesions on leaves; reduced photosynthesis and yield loss.
  • Rotate crops regularly to break the disease cycle.
  • Use resistant maize varieties to reduce susceptibility.
  • Apply compost tea to boost plant immunity.
  • Ensure adequate spacing between plants for better airflow.
  • Use neem oil spray as an organic antifungal treatment.
  • Apply a mixture of baking soda and water to slow fungal growth.
  • Encourage beneficial microbes by using organic soil amendments.
  • Use potassium bicarbonate as a natural fungicide.
  • Remove infected leaves and plant debris to prevent spread.
  • Mulch around plants to reduce soil splash contamination.
  • Avoid overhead irrigation to keep leaves dry.
  • Introduce Trichoderma-based biofungicides to suppress fungal spores.
  • Spray a garlic extract solution for its natural antifungal properties.
