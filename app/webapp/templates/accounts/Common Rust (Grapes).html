Crop Disease Organic Solutions

Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
Common Rust (Grapes) Reddish-brown powdery pustules on leaves; reduced fruit quality.
  • Apply sulfur-based organic fungicides.
  • Spray a mixture of baking soda and water.
  • Prune infected leaves to improve airflow.
  • Use neem oil as a preventive measure.
  • Apply copper-based fungicides every two weeks.
  • Encourage beneficial insects to control fungal spores.
  • Mulch around the base to prevent soil splash.
  • Avoid overhead irrigation to keep foliage dry.
  • Use potassium bicarbonate spray as a natural remedy.
  • Rotate crops to reduce rust buildup in soil.
  • Introduce biofungicides containing Trichoderma.
  • Maintain good vineyard hygiene by removing fallen leaves.
  • Spray compost tea to boost plant resistance.
