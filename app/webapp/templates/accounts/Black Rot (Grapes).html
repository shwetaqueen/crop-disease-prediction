Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
Black Rot (Grapes) Dark, circular lesions on leaves; shriveled, black fruit mummies.
  • Spray a mixture of baking soda and water.
  • Use sulfur-based organic fungicides.
  • Remove infected plant debris.
  • Prune and dispose of infected vines.
  • Improve air circulation by spacing plants properly.
  • Apply compost tea to strengthen plant immunity.
  • Use neem oil spray to prevent fungal spread.
  • Mulch around plants to reduce soil splash infections.
  • Introduce beneficial microbes to enhance soil health.
  • Rotate crops to minimize fungal persistence.
  • Use potassium bicarbonate as a natural antifungal spray.
  • Avoid overhead watering to keep foliage dry.
  • Apply a homemade garlic and chili extract spray.
