Disease Symptoms Organic Solutions
Downy Mildew (Watermelon) Yellow spots on leaves turning brown; fuzzy gray or purple mold on undersides.
  • Spray a mixture of baking soda and water.
  • Use neem oil to prevent fungal spread.
  • Apply copper-based organic fungicides.
  • Encourage good airflow by proper plant spacing.
  • Remove and destroy infected leaves immediately.
  • Use potassium bicarbonate spray as a natural remedy.
  • Apply compost tea to strengthen plant immunity.
  • Avoid overhead watering to keep foliage dry.
  • Introduce beneficial microbes like Trichoderma.
  • Rotate crops to prevent soil-borne mildew spores.
  • Spray a homemade garlic and chili extract solution.
  • Mulch around plants to reduce soil splash infections.
  • Use a milk spray (1:9 ratio of milk to water) to inhibit mildew.
